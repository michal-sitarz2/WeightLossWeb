About

This website aims to create an automated meal plan based on user preferences to aid with the weight loss. The premise of the system is for the user to try and lose around 1 kg per week by decreasing their calorie intake.
Having a BMI above healthy weight can be a cause to different health conditions, and the issue can be caused by many things. (More on Obesity)
Main course of action to decrease obesity is to eat a healthier, calorie-reduced diet, and exercise regularly (any form of activity 2.5 to 5 hours a week should be sufficient).
